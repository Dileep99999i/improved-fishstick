Our Story

More Than Fashion. It's a Movement.

Nice Snitch was born from a simple idea: to create a stage for the unsung heroes of Indian fashion. We travel the country, from bustling city studios to quiet village workshops, to find the most exciting, innovative, and authentic design voices.

Every piece on our platform is a "snitch"—a rare find, a hidden gem. When you shop with us, you're not just buying a piece of clothing; you're supporting an independent artist's dream and becoming part of their story. We believe in fashion that is conscious, crafted, and full of character.
